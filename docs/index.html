Hello!

My name is Abigail Leigh Bradford and I’ve been working as a professional baker in Fort Worth for about five years now.

After giving birth last year to my daughter, Charlotte Leigh, I decided to pursue my lifetime aspiration of owning my own business while staying home to raise her.

Leigh Baking Co. creates scratch baked goods that are familiar and cherished by you and your loved ones, but have a pastry chef’s touch. I have an eye for detail, strong creative vision, and an affinity for unique flavor combinations.

I look forward to getting to know y’all and sharing my craft with my community. I welcome any and all inquires in the DFW metroplex!
